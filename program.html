Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

Program Description The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration is a four-year course that prepares students for a career in an outcome-focused, technology-rich, professional environment. It familiarizes students with office practices, procedures, office systems, technology, teamwork, management skills, and principles of human relations and communication.
Career Opportunities • Court Stenographer | Captioner | Office Manager
• Executive Assistant | Receptionist | Event Organizer
• Administrative Assistant | Secretary | Bookkeeper
• Accounting Clerk | Office Clerk | Entrepreneur

Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education

Program Description A four-year teacher education program designed to equip future teachers with competencies in technical and vocational education. It aims to develop highly competent and motivated teachers in Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE) and their areas of specialization.
Career Opportunities • Grade 9-10 TLE Teachers
• Senior High School Tech-Voc Livelihood Track Teachers
• TVET Trainers/Instructors
• Higher Education Faculty Members
• TESDA Trainers and Assessors

Bachelor of Science in Agri-Business

Program Description A four-year program designed to prepare students for careers in the growing agribusiness sector. It covers production, processing, and distribution of food and fiber, focusing on export opportunities and agribusiness roles.
Career Opportunities • Corporate Planners | Entrepreneurs | Business Consultants
• Business Managers | Sales Representatives | Credit Analysts
• Instructors | Researchers | Extensionists

Bachelor of Public Administration

Program Description A four-year degree that provides students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in public sector management, policy development, labor relations, and good governance. It emphasizes policy analysis and leadership.
Career Opportunities • Technical Assistant
• Researcher in Government Agencies, NGOs, Academic Institutions
• Development Manager
• Executives in GOCCs, National/International Organizations
• Ambassador | Foreign Service Officer | Politician | College Instructor

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Security Management

Program Description Focuses on principles of security, risk management, and interventions to protect resources. Prepares graduates for supervisory roles in crime prevention, law enforcement, public safety, and security management.
Career Opportunities • Crime Prevention | Law Enforcement | Public Safety
• Security Management | Correctional Administration | PNP
• Security Manager | Consultant | AFP | PDEA
• Security Supervisor | Officer | Instructor | BFP Officer
• Private Security in Banks, Malls, Schools, Airports, Commercial Establishments
